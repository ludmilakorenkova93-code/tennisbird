Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tennis Bird handles information when you use the app. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

Tennis Bird does not require users to create an account. The app may access or process the following information only when it is needed for app features or content you choose to use:

  • Device network access, used to load online content and app configuration.
  • Location access, only after you grant permission through the Android system prompt.
  • Camera and microphone access, only after you grant permission through the Android system prompt.
  • Files or photos selected by you for upload or attachment features.
  • Clipboard text when you choose to copy, paste, read, or write clipboard content inside the app.
  • Basic app preferences stored locally on your device, such as game progress, selected ball, and the last opened page.

How We Use Information

Information is used to provide app functionality, remember your preferences, support media and file actions, and improve the reliability of the app experience. We do not sell personal information.

Third-Party Services

The app uses Firebase services to retrieve remote app configuration. Firebase may process technical data according to Google's privacy practices. You can review Google's privacy information at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Permissions

Android permissions are requested only when a feature needs them. You can allow or deny these permissions at any time in your device settings. Denying a permission may prevent some related features from working, but the rest of the app can still be used.

Data Storage

The app stores some settings locally on your device. This local data may include game settings, score information, selected ball type, and the last opened page. You can remove this data by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

Tennis Bird is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so the issue can be reviewed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the app publisher through the Google Play listing or the project repository at https://github.com/ludmilakorenkova93-code/tennisbird .
