Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tennis Bird handles information when you use the app. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

Information We Collect

Tennis Bird does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information from users.

  • Best score and basic game progress stored locally on your device.
  • Selected ball style and simple game preferences stored locally on your device.

How We Use Information

Local game data is used only to keep track of your progress, remember your preferences, and provide the core game experience. We do not sell personal information.

Data Storage

The app stores game settings, score information, and selected ball type locally on your device. You can remove this data by clearing the app data or uninstalling the app.

Children's Privacy

Tennis Bird is not intended to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so the issue can be reviewed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the app publisher through the Google Play listing or the project repository at https://github.com/ludmilakorenkova93-code/tennisbird .
